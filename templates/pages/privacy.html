Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 20, 2025

At Fixio, your privacy is our top priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Data: Name, email (only when creating an account)
  • File Data: Uploaded documents for tool processing — never stored longer than needed
  • Usage Data: For analytics and feature improvement

2. Data Usage & AI

Fixio processes your content using AI models only within your session. No document data is used for training or profiling. You control your files — we never claim ownership.

3. Security Practices

  • HTTPS end-to-end encryption
  • AES-256 encrypted storage (temporary)
  • Data auto-deletion after task completion (unless cloud save is enabled)

4. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and delete your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Export your information

5. Compliance With Legal Acts

Fixio complies with the following regulations:

  • GDPR (EU General Data Protection Regulation)
  • CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
  • DPDP Act (India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023)
  • LGPD (Brazil's General Data Protection Law)

6. Cookies & Analytics

We use minimal cookies for functionality. No third-party marketing trackers.

7. Children's Privacy

Fixio is not intended for users under 13 years of age.

8. Policy Updates

Users will be notified of any updates via email and on our homepage.
