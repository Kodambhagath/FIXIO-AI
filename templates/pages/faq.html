Privacy & Security

No. Fixio processes files in-memory and deletes them immediately after processing. Your files are never permanently stored on our servers. We automatically delete all uploaded files and generated results after 24 hours.

Yes, our AI features follow top industry standards for security. When using our AI tools, the content is processed securely and not used for training AI models. We prioritize your privacy and data security in all our services.

Your account information is encrypted and stored securely. We use industry-standard encryption methods and follow best practices for data protection. We never share your personal information with third parties without your consent.

We collect anonymous usage data to improve our services, but we don't track your personal activity. You can opt out of analytics in your account settings. For more information, please see our Privacy Policy.
